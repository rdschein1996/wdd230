Welcome to the Temple Inn & Suites!

Our goal is to help you attend the temple and we understand that it can be difficult to find the time and means to travel to the temple. This is especially true for those who plan longer trips, live further away, have a wedding to celebrate, or have a large group. We want to help you feel comfortable and able to attend the temple for whatever purpose you have. Our Inn & Suites are tailored to help!


We provide you with a home away from home while you attend the temple. We love our church history as well and want to share that with those who share our interest. Our locations allow us to share pieces of history as we also put together historic church site tours for groups and families that are interested. We welcome you to our home and hope your stay is an enjoyable one that allows you to appreciate the temple!
