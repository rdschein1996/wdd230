Target Audience

This site is directed toward people who care about planning outdoor activites and wish to know how the weather might have implications on their plans. Since Idaho has a lot of outdoor activities to do, we would look to target those who have interest in outdoor activities and need consistently updated information about the weather. Another target audience group would be those who travel and drive often such as truckers or those with commutes. We would also target local farmers who would be interested to determine how they need to take care of their crops and/or animals.
