Target Audience

This site is directed toward members of the LDS Church who wish to attend temples and have support for their trip. Sometimes it is difficult to get the temple and back home in one day and sometimes attending the temple can be for multiple purposes that span across multiple days. An example of this could be a wedding where the endowment takes place one day and the sealing takes place on another. Families and groups who wish to attend the temple are a primary target audience. Another target audience are full-time temple missionaries. Special suites are included at the hotels to accomodate a longer stay. We would also target youth leaders who plan temple and historical site visits for the youth.
