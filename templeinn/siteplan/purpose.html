Name


Temple Inn & Suites


Purpose


This site's purpose is provide information, services, and a place to stay for temple-goers. Four temples will have a nearby Inn. The four locations are Nauvoo, Winters Quarters, St. Louis, and Kansas City. Services and amenities include hotel rooms, free breakfast, indoor pool, fitness center, family search & history center, transportation shuttles to and from temples and sites, changing rooms, children's playroom, parking, Wi-Fi, wedding reception hall, and local church site tours. Each of the four locations are near meaningful church history sites so groups can sign up for site visits. Temple workers and missionaries also have access to long-term stay suites.
